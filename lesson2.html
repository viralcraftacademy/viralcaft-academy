Lesson 2: Viral Hooks (Stop the Scroll)

A hook is the first 1–3 seconds of your video. If it fails, the video fails.

🔥 3 Powerful Hook Types

  • "You’re doing this wrong..."
  • "Nobody talks about this..."
  • "I wish I knew this earlier..."

🧠 Why hooks matter

TikTok decides very early if people stay or leave your video. Strong hooks = higher watch time = more reach.

✍️ Your Task

Write 3 hook ideas for a TikTok video.
